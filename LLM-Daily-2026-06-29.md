- **Pricing**: Sol $5/$30, Terra $2.50/$15, Luna $1/$6 per million tokens. Cache writes at 1.25× uncached input rate; cache reads get 90% discount with 30-min minimum life.
- **Partnership**: Cerebras announced targeting up to 750 tokens/sec for Sol beginning July 2026.

### DeepSeek DSpark: Speculative Decoding, 85% Faster (June 27)
**Signal:** BREAKTHROUGH — A genuinely novel speculative decoding architecture that advances past EAGLE-3.
- **What it is**: Not a new model. A draft model + inference module attached to existing DeepSeek-V4 weights. MIT license.
- **Architecture**: Semi-autoregressive drafting (hybrid of parallel backbone + lightweight sequential Markov head at rank-256) + confidence-scheduled verification (hardware-aware prefix scheduler that dynamically trims verification length based on GPU load) + Zero-Overhead Scheduling (ZOS, async scheduling using prior two steps' predictions).
- **Performance**: 60–85% faster per-user generation on V4-Flash, 57–78% on V4-Pro vs. MTP-1 baseline. Acceptance length up 26.7–30.9% over EAGLE-3 on Qwen3 at 4B/8B/14B.
- **Open-source**: Full DeepSpec codebase for training/evaluating draft models on open models (Qwen3, Gemma). Checkpoints available on HF: `deepseek-ai/dspark_qwen3_*_block7`.
- **Paper**: "DSpark: Confidence-Scheduled Speculative Decoding with Semi-Autoregressive Generation" by Liang Wenfeng et al. at Peking University.
- **Sources**: [DeepSpec GitHub](https://github.com/deepseek-ai/DeepSpec) · [AcingAI deep dive](https://acingai.com/articles/deepseek-dspark-speculative-decoding) · [TechTimes](https://www.techtimes.com/articles/319236/20260628/deepseek-releases-dspark-speculative-decoding-makes-v4-85-percent-faster.htm) · [SCMP](https://www.scmp.com/tech/big-tech/article/3358647/faster-ai-lower-costs-dspark-eases-inference-bottlenecks-and-chip-strain-says-deepseek)
