Google has published the **Antigravity CLI (agy)** — a Go-written terminal agent that replaces `gemini-cli` entirely. The old CLI (104K+ stars, Apache-2.0) reaches end-of-life on **June 18, 2026** — just six days away. agy is compiled in Go, starts in milliseconds, and includes a sub-agent orchestrator for parallel execution.

**Key details:**
