### F04: Higgs Audio v3 TTS — 4B Conversational Speech Model (SIGNAL)
- **What:** Boson AI released Higgs Audio v3 TTS on June 4, 2026. A 4B-parameter autoregressive decoder built on a Qwen3-4B backbone, designed for conversational voice agents with inline control over emotion, style, prosody, and sound effects mid-stream.
- **Details:**
     - Single-digit WER/CER on 102 languages (85 at production quality). Supports 24 kHz audio at 25 fps with 8 codebooks.
     - Control tokens: `|<emotion:elation|>`, `|<prosody:speed_very_slow|>`, `|<sfx:laughter|>` — all combinable mid-utterance.
     - Outperforms OmniVoice, Fish Audio S2 Pro, Qwen3-TTS-1.7B, IndexTTS-2, MOSS-TTS-v1.5 on conversational benchmarks (Emergent TTS).
     - Weights available on Hugging Face (`bosonai/higgs-audio-v3-tts-4b`) under research/non-commercial license.
     - End-to-end serving on SGLang-Omni announced by LMSYS for real-time inference.
     - Source: [Boson AI blog](https://www.boson.ai/blog/higgs-audio-v3-tts), [Hugging Face](https://huggingface.co/bosonai/higgs-audio-v3-tts-4b), [LMSYS Blog](https://www.lmsys.org/blog/2026-06-04-higgs-audio-v3-tts/)
- **Signal:** A genuinely competitive open TTS model for conversational use.
