### F01: Google Gemma 4 Family — 12B Open Multimodal, E4B/E2B Edge Quantizations (BREAKTHROUGH)
- **What:** Google DeepMind released the full Gemma 4 family on June 3, 2026. The headline is **Gemma 4 12B**, a dense encoder-free multimodal model designed to run on consumer laptops with ~16 GB RAM. But the full family includes 26B (MoE), 31B (MoE), plus E4B and E2B "effective parameter" quantizations optimized for edge/IoT devices.
- **Details:**
   - **Architecture:** Encoder-free unified decoder-only transformer. Vision flows through a 35M-parameter vision module; audio enters raw 16 kHz directly into the token space — no separate encoders. This reduces memory overhead, latency, and deployment complexity.
   - **Benchmarks (from deepmind.google/models/gemma/gemma-4/):** Gemma 4 31B IT: MMLU Multilingual 85.2%, AIME 2026 89.2%, GPQA Diamond 84.3%, LiveCodeBench v6 80.0%. Gemma 4 26B A4B: MMLU Pro 76.9%, τ2-bench (agentic tool use) 85.5%. Gemma 4 12B (from blog post): GPQA Diamond 78.8%, MMLU Pro 77.2%, LiveCodeBench v6 72%, DocVQA 94.9%, AIME 2026 77.5%.
   - **Context:** 256K tokens for larger models; LiteRT-LM supports up to 32K.
   - **License:** Apache 2.0.
   - **Availability:** Weights on HF (`google/gemma-4-12B-it`, `google/gemma-4-E2B-it`). Ollama libraries live (`gemma4:e4b`, `gemma4:e2b`). MLX support via Google AI Edge Gallery (macOS app). LiteRT-LM format for on-device serving. llama.cpp, vLLM, SGLang, Unsloth all supported.
   - **M4 Max feasibility:** The LiteRT-LM card reports 6,235 MB model size, ~7,763 MB GPU memory on M4 GPU, 29.56 tok/s decode, 243.55 tok/s prefills. Karl's 128GB M4 Max will run this with room to spare — 30 tok/s is solid for agentic workflows.
- **Sources:** [deepmind.google/models/gemma/gemma-4/](https://deepmind.google/models/gemma/gemma-4/), [blog.google intro post](https://blog.google/innovation-and-ai/technology/developers-tools/introducing-gemma-4-12b/), [developers.googleblog.com local workflow guide](https://developers.googleblog.com/bringing-gemma-4-12b-to-your-laptop-unlocking-local-agentic-workflows-with-google-ai-edge/), [HF LiteRT-LM card](https://huggingface.co/litert-community/gemma-4-12B-it-litert-lm), [ollama.com gemma4:e4b](https://ollama.com/library/gemma4:e4b), [the-decoder.com deep dive](https://the-decoder.com/google-deepminds-gemma-4-12b-squeezes-multimodal-ai-onto-a-laptop-with-just-16-gb-of-ram/)
